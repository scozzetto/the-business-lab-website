Module 10

Activity Logs

4 min read

The activity log records every significant action in the system — who did what, when, and to whom. It's your audit trail for security, compliance, and troubleshooting.

Viewing Activity Logs

  1. Go to Settings → Activity Log
    Navigate to the Activity Log section (manager/admin only).
  2. Browse or Filter
    The log shows recent activities in reverse chronological order. Use filters to narrow down.

What Gets Logged

CategoryExamples
Client changesCreated, edited, deleted, restored client records
PaymentsProcessed, refunded, voided payments
BookingsCreated, modified, cancelled appointments
Staff changesAdded, deactivated, role changed
SystemLogin/logout, settings changes, exports

Filtering the Log

  • By staff member — See all actions by a specific person
  • By action type — See only payments, only edits, etc.
  • By date range — Narrow to a specific time period
  • By client — See all actions related to a specific client

Logs cannot be edited or deleted. This ensures the integrity of the audit trail. Even admins cannot modify log entries.
