Module 8

Payment Reports

4 min read

Payment reports break down transactions by payment method, status, and time period. Use them to reconcile cash, verify card deposits, and track failed payments.

Generating a Payment Report

  1. Go to Reports → Payments
    Navigate to the Payment Reports section.
  2. Set Date Range
    Choose the period to analyze.
  3. Filter by Method (Optional)
    View all methods or filter to just Card, Cash, or Check.
  4. View Results
    See totals per method, successful vs. failed, and individual transactions.

Report Sections

SectionWhat It Shows
SummaryTotal payments, count, average transaction
By methodCard, cash, check totals with percentages
Failed paymentsDeclined cards, insufficient funds, errors
Transaction listEvery individual payment with details

Review failed payments weekly. Clients with failed card payments may have outstanding balances. Follow up to collect payment.
