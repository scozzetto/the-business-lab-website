Module 2

Family Relationships

3 min read

Link client records as family members to manage household accounts, shared billing, and family packages. Each person keeps their own individual record but is connected to their relatives.

Linking Family Members

  1. Open a Client Profile
    Open the profile of one family member (e.g., the parent).
  2. Find the Family Section
    Scroll down to the "Family & Relationships" section of the profile.
  3. Click + Link Family Member
    Click the button and search for the other family member by name, email, or client ID.
  4. Set the Relationship
    Choose the relationship type: Spouse, Parent, Child, Sibling, or Other. The link is created in both directions — if you add a "Child" link on the parent's profile, the child's profile automatically shows a "Parent" link back.

Relationship Types

TypeInverseUse Case
SpouseSpouseMarried/partnered couples
ParentChildParent paying for child's services
ChildParentMinor child of client
SiblingSiblingBrothers/sisters
OtherOtherCaretakers, guardians, etc.

Household Benefits

When clients are linked as family:

  • Shared billing — One family member can pay for another's services
  • Family view — See all family members from any linked profile
  • Family packages — If your business offers family pricing, linked accounts are eligible

Removing a Family Link

To unlink family members, open either person's profile, find the relationship in the Family section, and click Remove Link. This removes the connection in both directions.
