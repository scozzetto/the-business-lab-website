Front Desk Dashboard

5 min read

The front desk dashboard gives you a real-time overview of your day — who's coming in, who's checked in, pending tasks, and today's key numbers. It refreshes automatically.

Dashboard Sections

  1. Today's Stats Bar
    At the top, see at-a-glance numbers: scheduled appointments, checked-in clients, walk-ins today, and revenue collected.
  2. Appointment Queue
    A list of today's appointments sorted by time. Each entry shows client name, service, provider, and status (Scheduled, Checked In, In Progress, Complete).
  3. Check-In Feed
    Real-time feed showing clients as they check in. New check-ins appear at the top with a notification sound (if enabled).
  4. Quick Actions
    Buttons for common tasks: Check In Client, New Walk-In, New Task, Quick Payment.

Auto-Refresh

The dashboard refreshes every 30 seconds to show the latest data. You'll see new check-ins and status changes without manually reloading the page.

Keep it open. The front desk dashboard is designed to stay open all day on a dedicated screen or tab. It's your real-time view of everything happening at your location.
