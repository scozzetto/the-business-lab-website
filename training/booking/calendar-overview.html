Calendar Overview

4 min read

The booking calendar shows all appointments across your business. Switch between views and filter by provider to manage your schedule.

Calendar Views

ViewBest ForHow to Access
DayDetailed hourly view of one dayClick Day tab
WeekPlanning ahead, seeing patternsClick Week tab
MonthBig picture overview, busy daysClick Month tab

Reading the Calendar

  1. Color Coding
    Each service provider has a color. Appointments are colored by provider, making it easy to see each person's schedule at a glance.
  2. Time Slots
    In Day and Week views, the vertical axis shows time (typically 8 AM to 8 PM). Appointments appear as blocks proportional to their duration.
  3. Clicking an Appointment
    Click any appointment block to see details: client name, service, provider, time, and status. You can edit or cancel from here.

Navigation

  • Previous / Next — Arrow buttons move forward or backward by one day, week, or month
  • Today — Click Today to jump back to the current date
  • Filter by Provider — Use the provider dropdown to show only one provider's appointments

Tip: Double-click an empty time slot to quickly create a new booking at that time.
