All credit card and electronic payment processing for Client's business operations shall be conducted exclusively through The Business Lab's Stripe payment infrastructure. Client acknowledges and agrees that:

7. Setup, Training & Onboarding
  • Initial system setup and configuration is included at no additional cost.
  • One (1) onboarding call is included to walk Client through the platform and configure initial settings.
  • Additional training sessions beyond the initial onboarding call shall be billed at the hourly rate specified in Section 10.
  • Provider will deploy the platform to Client's branded subdomain (e.g., clientname.thebusiness-lab.com) within ten (10) business days of Agreement execution.
8. Support
  • Provider shall offer email and phone support during standard business hours (Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM ET).
  • Support requests will receive an initial response within one (1) business day.
  • Critical system issues (platform down, payment processing failure) will be addressed with best-effort urgency, targeting resolution within four (4) hours during business hours.
  • Provider targets a platform uptime of 99.5% measured on a monthly basis, excluding scheduled maintenance windows communicated at least 24 hours in advance.

11. Termination
  • Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this Agreement at the end of the then-current term by providing thirty (30) days' prior written notice.
  • Early Termination by Client: If Client terminates this Agreement before the end of the Initial Term or any renewal term, Client shall pay an early termination fee equal to fifty percent (50%) of the remaining monthly fees due for the balance of the then-current term.
  • Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach.
  • Effect of Termination: Upon termination, Client's access to the platform will be disabled, all Provider-owned hardware must be returned, and any outstanding fees become immediately due and payable.
