Getting Started

  1. Log into your portal — Visit your portal URL above and sign in with your temporary credentials. You'll be prompted to set a new password.
  2. Explore your dashboard — Familiarize yourself with the main dashboard. Each module is accessible from the sidebar navigation.
  3. Connect your Stripe account — Navigate to Settings and follow the prompts to connect your Stripe account for payment processing.
  4. Add your staff — Go to Staff Management and create accounts for your team members. Assign roles and permissions as needed.
  5. Configure your menu or services — Set up your products, services, and pricing in the appropriate module.
  6. Test a transaction — Run a test order through the POS or kiosk to ensure everything is connected and working properly.
  7. Schedule your training session — Contact us to schedule additional training for you and your team if needed.
